ABOUT US

MaxxInk is a leading North American supplier of ink and toner products, both factory-original and recycled. We provide products and services for organizations of all sizes as well as individuals. By purchasing through MaxxInk, you will save 30-60% on Ink and Toner purchases with our environmentally friendly, Made in The USA cartridges.
SAVINGS

Ink and toner are the largest office supply expense globally and office printing contines to grow at a rate of 10% each year. MaxxInk’s consultative approach will not only analyze usage for your entire printer fleet, but also demonstrates significant realized cost savings. Contact us today so we can discuss how the MaxxInk process can work for you!

QUALITY

MaxxInk warrants all products to be free of defects due to workmanship. Additionally, we warrant that if under normal use, our products have been the source of damage to your equipment, we will reimburse you for the cost of repairs or replacement. All our imaging supplies meet or exceed OEM standards. If for any reason you are dissatisfied, we will credit 100% of the purchase price.

CONVENIENCE

Converting to premium compatible cartridges not only offers substantial savings, but is a seamless process that we walk you through step-by-step. Our online ordering system offers a customized, user-friendly and secure environment. The MaxxInk proces will guide you to the best products suited to your organization’s specific needs without sacrificing quality or value.

LOCAL SUPPORT

At MaxxInk.com, we believe in giving back to the community!
That’s why we’re proud sponsors of the Clarity Newhouse Racing “Mobile Science Lab.”

Enter promo code RACING when you place your order and we will donate 10% of the revenue to Clarity Newhouse Racing!

Proud Sponsor of Clarity Newhouse Racing and the Race Car Mobile Science Lab

Race car driver Clarity Newhouse created the Race Car Mobile Science Lab to inspire students to consider
careers in science and introduce them to rewarding opportunities available in automotive and engineering fields.

This exciting educational program brings a race car to schools so students can explore science,
technology, engineering and math during a fun, hands-on science class taught by a female race car driver.

When you shop at MaxxInk.com and enter promo code RACING you're helping create
wonderful educational opportunities for students by supporting Clarity Newhouse Racing!
